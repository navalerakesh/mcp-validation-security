# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for everyone contributing to AI security and safety.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards others

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Harassment, discriminatory language, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting comments, or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Any conduct inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Project maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.
